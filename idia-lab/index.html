IDIA Lab

davejrodriguez
November 7, 2016

One of my first large projects at the Institute for Digital Intermedia Art (IDIA Lab) was the migration and redesign of the institute's website. The institute used this site to publicize their projects and market their services to potential clients. Though a part of Ball State University, most of their contracts came from outside the university and a strong web presence was essential.

The site had first been built with Ruby on Rails, but only the initial developer knew how to add and change content. Seeing this, I knew a proper content management system would be beneficial to the lab's designers and editors. My job was to recreate the design in a WordPress theme, then migrate the site and domain name to JustHost. Five years later, IDIA Lab is still effectively using the site, content management system, and theme I set up.
