A virtual social installation by John Fillwalk, IDIA Lab and Jody Naderi, CAP<\/p>\n\n\n\n

This work, designed specifically for Nuit Blanche Toronto activates a locative field in which participants engage in dynamic social experience. Individual users of the app interact with a dynamic feedback system allowing them to find the cadence of a contemplative walk. Participants can view other app users on a live map \u2013 locating them spatially to join in a unique group or virtual room \u2013 forming a dynamic choir of collaborative synchronous light and music.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
