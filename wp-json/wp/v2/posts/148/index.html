IDIA Lab \u2013 in collaboration with BSU Assistant Professor of Architecture, Kristin Barry \u2013 has  designed the cultural heritage mobile application, Virtual Columbus Experience \u2013 allowing users to virtually tour the rich architectural history of Columbus, Indiana. This locative mobile app was produced with fellowship support of Ball State University\u2019s Digital Scholarship Lab<\/a>.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Though a relatively small city, Columbus has made itself a cradle of modern architecture, commissioning many buildings and public works since the middle of the 20th century. The number of landmark buildings and notable architects to have worked in the city has earned it the nickname \u201cAthens on the Prairie.\u201d<\/p>\n\n\n\n
