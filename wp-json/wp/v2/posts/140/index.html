Meet3D is a virtual collaboration space that brings people together to share, learn, and work \u2013 wherever they are. Private or public auditoriums, classrooms, conference halls or offices can be joined by any group or audience on demand. Meet3D was developed by the Institute for Digital Intermedia Arts at Ball State University to bring remote participants together in a common space and facilitate a sense of physical presence. The platform is available in both browser-based and executable versions.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
